## Description
TheCardLab is the AI command center for sports card collectors.

GRADE LAB — Snap a card, get an instant PSA / BGS / SGC / CGC prediction. Centering, defects, edges, surfaces. Post-grade value comp in seconds.

DEAL SCREENER — Paste an eBay, PWCC, Goldin or auction URL. We compute grading probability + ROI instantly.

PORTFOLIO — Live cost-basis tracking, gain/loss windows (24h, 7d, 30d, 1y), comp-driven valuation.

GRADING TRACKER — Manage PSA/BGS/SGC/CGC submissions, status sync, ETAs.

WANTLIST — Save what you hunt. Alerts when matches hit.

MARKETPLACE — Find cards through unified search across major venues.

VAULT — Off-site storage with $25M insurance, zero-sales-tax intra-vault trades, one-tap marketplace listing.

Built for hobbyists, breakers, dealers, investors. Whether you collect vintage, ultra-modern, Pokémon, soccer, basketball — TheCardLab gives you the analytics edge.
