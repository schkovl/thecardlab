## Full Description
TheCardLab is the AI command center for sports card collectors.

🔍 GRADE LAB
Snap a card. Get an instant PSA / BGS / SGC / CGC grade prediction with centering, defect, edge and surface analysis. Post-grade value comp in seconds.

🛒 DEAL SCREENER
Paste any eBay, PWCC, Goldin or auction URL. We compute grading probability + ROI instantly.

📊 PORTFOLIO
Live cost-basis tracking, gain/loss windows (24h, 7d, 30d, 1y), comp-driven valuation.

📦 GRADING TRACKER
Manage PSA/BGS/SGC/CGC submissions. Status sync, ETAs, post-grade ROI.

🎯 WANTLIST
Save the cards you're hunting. Get alerts when matches hit.

🛍️ MARKETPLACE
Unified search across major venues.

🔒 VAULT
Off-site storage with $25M insurance, zero-sales-tax intra-vault trades.

Built for hobbyists, breakers, dealers and investors.
